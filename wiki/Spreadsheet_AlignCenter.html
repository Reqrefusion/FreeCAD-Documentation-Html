Description

The Spreadsheet AlignCenter tool aligns the contents of selected cells to the center horizontally.

Usage

  1. Make sure there is an active spreadsheet.
  2. Select one or more cells.
  3. There are several ways to invoke the tool:
    • Press the Align center button.
    • Select the Spreadsheet → Alignment → Align center option from the menu.
