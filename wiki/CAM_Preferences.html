Overview

The preferences screen of the CAM Workbench are found in the Preferences Editor, Edit → Preferences → CAM.

There are four tabs: Job preferences, Dressups, GUI and Advanced.

Job preferences

There are four sections: General, Post processor, Setup and Tools.
