NAMeetup/2024/Springfield/Food

Here are a few suggestions for food & drink for visitors to Springfield, Illinois for the FreeCAD 2024 North American Meetup.

The local specialty, the "horseshoe", is an open-faced sandwich topped with fries and rarebit/cheese sauce. It is served at many locations. Try one while you're here!

Visit Springfield offers a more complete listing for local food and also one for breweries & wineries.
